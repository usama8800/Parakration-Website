The registration process

  1. Fill in your team's details in the form below. Make sure that you write down all the required details, and that no important information is left out.
  2. Each delegate will participate in five events. Episteme and Ideas in Action are compulsory. Three events will be of your choice.
  3. One delegation can have a maximum of six delegates.
  4. Once you press the register button, a 'thank you' message should appear on your screen.
  5. For questions and queries about accomodation for participants from schools / institutes outside of lahore, contact Raheem Zafar at 03008462977

Points to remember

  1. Each delegation can only have ONE head delegate.
  2. The deadline for submitting the form is 10th November.
  3. The delegation fee and the participation fee for all delegates must be paid at LGS Paragon. The school office will be open for delegates to visit on work days between the hours of 8 a.m and 4 p.m.
  4. The head delegate must be in contact with the registration team at the time of payment.
